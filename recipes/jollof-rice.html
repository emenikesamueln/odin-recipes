Nigerian Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a popular and common food in Nigeria and Africa at large

It's best served hot and many times taken with soft drinks

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Fresh pepper
  • Maggi
  • Groundnut oil

Steps

  1. Parboil the rice for about three minutes
  2. Fry the oil and add sliced onions
  3. Add grounded tomatoes, onions and other ingredients
  4. Add the parboiled rice and cook for about 30 minutes
