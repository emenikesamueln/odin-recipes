Abacha

Abacha is a local recipe in the eastern part of Nigerian

It is easy to prepare and is highly palatable

Ingredients

  • Dried sliced cassava
  • Palm oil
  • Garden egg leave
  • Ogiri
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Soak the dried sliced cassava in water for about 30 minutes
  2. Put red oil in a mortar, add the other ingredients and mix thoroughly
  3. Add the cassava and mix properly
